I'm Deslavi Vibes

I officially kicked off my music carrer in 2018 with the release of a mashup on youtube titled "Aika". I started of as a wordsmith and later diversified my skills to a more dynamic artist which allows me to wing between bongo and afrobongo to express the extent fo my versatility. Mostly I base my music on love and encouraging people, in a way giving them hope as they cope with the changing times. I hope to impact on peoples lives with music in its purest form.
